The Big Bend 100



Here is a description of the 100 mile hike from Big Bend Ranch State park through Big Bend National in south west Texas.

Don't die, that's not cool. Know how to read a map, clean your own water, and pace yourelf. The suggested dates to complete the 100 miles is November-January 31 and moveing from West to East
